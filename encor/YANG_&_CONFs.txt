====== YANG & CONFs ======
Created Friday 15 September 2023

YANG is a format to represent information about something
You can collect the information necessary to fill out that YANG data model, and then encode that data in JSON or XML

CONFs send data encoded in JSON or XML, but formatted like the YANG data model, making it so theoretically you could
	represent the same thing in any markup language every.

Your teacher wants an essay
	Essay formatting (1 into paragraph, 3 body paragraphs, and 1 closing paragraph) is YANG
		it is a standardized way to structure the data
	Choosing to share as a PDF, MS Word .docx, or Google Doc is NETCONF and RESTCONF
